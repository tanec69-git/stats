Level of Measurement Practice App

Classify the variable into one of the following:
  • Nominal
  • Ordinal
  • Interval
  • Ratio
Helpful Tips:

• Nominal → names, labels, identifiers only.

• Ordinal → order matters.

• Interval → equal intervals but no true zero.

• Ratio → has a true zero and can multiply/divide meaningfully.
