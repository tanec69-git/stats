Conditional Probability Practice

The probability that a student will study hard for his examination is 0.72, and the probability that he will study hard and score distinction is 0.68.

What is the probability that the student, if studied hard for his examination, will score distinction?

Let:

\[ H = \text{student studies hard} \]
\[ D = \text{student scores distinction} \]

Given:

\[ P(H)=0.72 \]
\[ P(H \cap D)=0.68 \]

We want to find:

\[ P(D|H) \]

Use the conditional probability formula:

\[ P(D|H)=\frac{P(H \cap D)}{P(H)} \]
\[ =\frac{0.68}{0.72} \]
\[ \approx 0.9444 \]

Answer:

\[ \boxed{0.9444} \]
