5. An e-commerce company monitors the delivery times (in days) of its parcels. Based on historical data, the company observes that the shortest delivery time is 1 day and longest delivery time is 7 days. The delivery time of a randomly selected parcel is \(X\) days.

The probability density function is given by:

$$ f(x) = \begin{cases} \frac{1}{6}(x - 1), & 1 \le x \le 3 \\ \frac{1}{12}(7 - x), & 3 < x \le 7 \end{cases} $$

(a) Show that this probability density function (PDF) is valid.
(b) Find \(P(2 \le X \le 6)\).
(c) "The probability increases from day 1 to day 3, but decreases from day 3 to day 7." Explain what this indicates about the delivery times in context.
(d) Find the probability that delivery takes more than 5 days.
(e) Find the probability that the delivery arrives within 2 days.
