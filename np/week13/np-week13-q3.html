3. The weight loss (in kg) after a week's slimming programme is a random variable \(X\) that has the following density function:

$$ f(x) = \begin{cases} 0.2x + 0.6, & \text{for } 1 < x < 2 \\ 0.1, & \text{for } 2 \le x < 3 \\ 0, & \text{otherwise} \end{cases} $$

Sketch the above probability density function graph and show that the area under the graph is equal to 1.

Find the probability that the weight loss of a person who goes through the programme to be:
   (i) at least 1.5kg
   (ii) at most 2.5kg
   (iii) between 1.8 to 2.8kg
