Composite Function

Determine the composite function \(gh(x)\).

\[ g(x)=\sqrt{2x+4} \] \[ h(x)=2x-7 \]
Step 1: \[ gh(x)=g(h(x)) \]
Step 2: Substitute \(h(x)=2x-7\) into \(g(x)\). Since \[ g(x)=\sqrt{2x+4} \] replace \(x\) with \((2x-7)\): \[ gh(x)=\sqrt{2(2x-7)+4} \]
Step 3: Simplify. \[ gh(x)=\sqrt{4x-14+4} \] \[ gh(x)=\sqrt{4x-10} \]
\[ gh(x)=\sqrt{4x-10} \]
