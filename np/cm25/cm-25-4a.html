📚 Elective Combination Choices 🎓

Learn the multiplication principle using elective selections!
In her course, Jenny must choose one elective each from three categories:

• 🎨 Arts (4 choices)
• 💻 Technology (5 choices)
• 🌎 Languages (3 choices)

How many different elective combinations can she make?

🎯 Step 1: Identify the Number of Choices

Jenny must choose:
🎨 Arts

4 Choices
💻 Technology

5 Choices
🌎 Languages

3 Choices

✖️ Step 2: Apply the Multiplication Principle

Since Jenny chooses one elective from EACH category, multiply the number of choices together.
Total Combinations \[ = 4 \times 5 \times 3 \]

🧮 Step 3: Calculate the Total

\[ 4 \times 5 = 20 \] \[ 20 \times 3 = 60 \]
✅ Final Answer: \[ \boxed{60} \]
