🍎 Fruit Combination Problem 🍊

Practice combinations using fruits! 🥭🍐
A fruit carton contains 14 fruits: 🍎 3 apples, 🍊 5 oranges, 🥭 2 mangoes and 🍐 4 pears.

If the order in which the fruits are picked does not matter, in how many ways can 8 fruits be selected to include:
(i) any type of fruits,
(ii) all the oranges and mangoes,
(iii) only apples, mangoes and pears,
(iv) at least 4 oranges.

🍉 (i) Any type of fruits

Select any 8 fruits from 14: \[ {}^{14}C_8 \] \[ = 3003 \]
✅ Answer: \(3003\)

🍊🥭 (ii) All the oranges and mangoes

All oranges and mangoes must be included: \[ 5 + 2 = 7 \text{ fruits} \] Need 1 more fruit from apples and pears: \[ 3 + 4 = 7 \text{ fruits} \] Number of ways: \[ {}^7C_1 \] \[ = 7 \]
✅ Answer: \(7\)

🍎🥭🍐 (iii) Only apples, mangoes and pears

Exclude all oranges 🍊 Total available fruits: \[ 3 + 2 + 4 = 9 \] Select 8 fruits: \[ {}^9C_8 \] \[ = 9 \]
✅ Answer: \(9\)

🍊🍊🍊🍊 (iv) At least 4 oranges

Cases:

Case 1: Exactly 4 oranges \[ {}^5C_4 \times {}^9C_4 \] \[ = 5 \times 126 = 630 \]
Case 2: Exactly 5 oranges \[ {}^5C_5 \times {}^9C_3 \] \[ = 1 \times 84 = 84 \]
Total ways: \[ 630 + 84 = 714 \]
✅ Answer: \(714\)
