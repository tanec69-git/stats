Probability Question

A bag contains 7 black balls and 5 blue balls. What is the probability of selecting a random group of 4 black balls and 3 blue balls from the bag?
Total number of balls: \[ 7 + 5 = 12 \]
Number of favourable selections: \[ {}^7C_4 \times {}^5C_3 \]
Total possible selections: \[ {}^{12}C_7 \]
Probability: \[ P= \frac{{}^7C_4 \times {}^5C_3} {{}^{12}C_7} \]
Calculate: \[ = \frac{35 \times 10}{792} \]
Final Answer: \[ \boxed{ \frac{175}{396} } \]
