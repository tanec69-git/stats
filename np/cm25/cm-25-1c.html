Venn Diagram Survey App

A group of \(120\) polytechnic students completed a survey on the drama genre which they liked.

The survey showed that:
  • \(54\) liked Romantic Comedies
  • \(66\) liked Crime Thrillers
  • \(19\) liked both Romantic Comedies and Crime Thrillers
  • \(24\) liked both Romantic Comedies and Science Fiction
  • \(55\) liked both Science Fiction and Crime Thrillers
  • \(10\) liked all \(3\) drama genres surveyed
  • \(7\) did not like any of the \(3\) drama genres surveyed
Complete the Venn diagram to show the preference data above.
RC: Romantic Comedies     CT: Crime Thrillers     SF: Science Fiction
Figure 1(c)
Step 1: Place the centre region first. \[ RC \cap CT \cap SF = 10 \]
Step 2: Find the pair-only regions. \[ RC \cap CT \text{ only} = 19 - 10 = 9 \] \[ RC \cap SF \text{ only} = 24 - 10 = 14 \] \[ CT \cap SF \text{ only} = 55 - 10 = 45 \]
Step 3: Find RC only. \[ 54 - (9 + 14 + 10) = 21 \]
Step 4: Find CT only. \[ 66 - (9 + 45 + 10) = 2 \]
Step 5: Students inside the circles: \[ 120 - 7 = 113 \] Add known regions: \[ 21 + 2 + 9 + 14 + 45 + 10 = 101 \] Therefore, \[ SF \text{ only} = 113 - 101 = 12 \]
(i) Science Fiction

\[ 12 + 14 + 45 + 10 = 81 \] Therefore, \[ \boxed{81} \]
(ii) Romantic Comedies or Science Fiction but not Crime Thrillers

Required regions:
  • RC only = \(21\)
  • SF only = \(12\)
  • \(RC \cap SF\) only = \(14\)
\[ 21 + 12 + 14 = 47 \] Therefore, \[ \boxed{47} \]
(iii) Only one drama genre

Add the single-only regions: \[ 21 + 2 + 12 = 35 \] Therefore, \[ \boxed{35} \]
