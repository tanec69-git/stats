Venn Diagram Shading App

Shade the region(s) corresponding to the following expression:

\[ A \cap B' \cap C' \]
Figure 1(b)
Explanation:

\[ A \cap B' \cap C' \] means the elements that are:
  • inside \(A\)
  • outside \(B\)
  • outside \(C\)
Therefore, the shaded region is the part of \(A\) excluding all regions inside \(B\) and \(C\), including their overlap.
