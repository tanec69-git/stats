Committee Formation App

Problem

A committee of 4 persons is to be selected from:
👩

Women

5 women

👨

Men

6 men

👥

Committee

4 persons


The committee must contain both genders.

Find the number of committees if:
  1. More men than women
  2. At least 1 woman
  3. A particular woman is selected

(i) More men than women

Since the committee has 4 people, "more men than women" means:

3 men and 1 woman

Choose:

  • 3 men from 6 men
  • 1 woman from 5 women
6 C 3 × 5 C 1
= 20 × 5
= 100
100 committees

(ii) At least 1 woman

Possible cases:

1W & 3M
2W & 2M
3W & 1M

Use the Addition Principle.

5 C 1 × 6 C 3
+
5 C 2 × 6 C 2
+
5 C 3 × 6 C 1
= 100 + 150 + 60
= 310
310 committees

(iii) A particular woman is selected

Let the particular woman be P.

P + 3 men
P + 2 men + 1 woman
P + 1 man + 2 women
1 × 6 C 3
+
1 × 6 C 2 × 4 C 1
+
1 × 6 C 1 × 4 C 2
= 20 + 60 + 36
= 116
116 committees
