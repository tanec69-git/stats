Camera Selection Combination App

Problem

In a box of digital cameras in a factory:
📷

Total Cameras

12 cameras

✅

Good Cameras

9 cameras

❌

Defective Cameras

3 cameras

🛒

Selection

Choose 4 cameras


Find the number of ways if:
  1. There are no restrictions
  2. There must not be any defective cameras
  3. The selection must include exactly 2 defective cameras
  4. The selection must include no more than 2 defective cameras

(i) No Restrictions

Choose any 4 cameras from 12 cameras
12 C 4
= 495
495 ways

(ii) No Defective Cameras

Choose all 4 cameras from the 9 good cameras
9 C 4
= 126
126 ways

(iii) Exactly 2 Defective Cameras

Choose:

2 defective cameras from 3
and
2 good cameras from 9
3 C 2 × 9 C 2
= 3 × 36
= 108
108 ways

(iv) No More Than 2 Defective Cameras

Possible cases:

0 defective cameras
1 defective camera
2 defective cameras

Use the Addition Principle.

3 C 0 × 9 C 4
+
3 C 1 × 9 C 3
+
3 C 2 × 9 C 2
= 126 + 252 + 108
= 486
486 ways
