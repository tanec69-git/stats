Revision Exercise 1: Sets and Relations

Question 1 (b)

Use a Venn diagram to shade each of the following sets:

(i) $(A \cap B) \cup C$

Step-by-step logic:

  1. Identify the region representing $A \cap B$ (the overlapping area between Set A and Set B).
  2. Identify the entire region of Set C.
  3. Combine these areas together. Shade everything inside C, plus the intersection of A and B.

(ii) $(A^{\prime} \cap B^{\prime}) \cup C^{\prime}$

Visual Step-by-Step Construction:

Instead of relying on set algebra, we can find the final region by visually building up the components of the expression and combining the shaded areas.

Step 1: $A^{\prime}$

Shade everything outside of Set A.

Step 2: $B^{\prime}$

Shade everything outside of Set B.

Step 3: $A^{\prime} \cap B^{\prime}$

Find the intersection (overlapping shades) of Step 1 and Step 2.

Step 4: $C^{\prime}$

Shade everything outside of Set C.

Final Solution

Union ($\cup$) of Step 3 and Step 4: Combine all shaded regions together. The only unshaded part remaining is the section of C that intersects with A or B.
